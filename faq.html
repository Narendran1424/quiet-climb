Coaching and mentoring are not the same:

  • Mentoring is about advice and lessons from someone who has walked the path before you.

What makes The Quiet Climb unique is that I bring both. As a senior global aerospace leader, I mentor by sharing lived experience. As a coach, I help you find your own authentic path.
